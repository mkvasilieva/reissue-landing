Fair questions.

How much control do I have?
All of it. Reissue never writes for you — if you didn't make it, it won't post it. You have the final edit on everything: tweak it, rewrite it, or flag what's off and it adjusts. And nothing publishes without your click. Email never auto-sends, ever.
How does it sound like me?
It learns your voice from what you've already published, then holds to it. If something sounds off, you flag it, and it adjusts — sounding more like you each time. You approve everything before it goes anywhere.
How does it know what works on each platform?
Reissue keeps up with the current best practices for every platform it supports — formats, structure, hooks, captions, the lot — and updates them as the platforms change. So your content isn't just reformatted, it's shaped to how each channel rewards content right now, not the way it did a year ago.
What do I actually give it?
One real video and a little about your brand. It doesn't need to be polished or made for us — a talking-head, a voiceover, something you already posted, it all works. No scripts, no prompts, no blank page.
Which platforms do you support?
At launch: email, Instagram and TikTok. More are on the way — YouTube, LinkedIn and X are next — and founding members help decide the order.
Is my content mine? Do you train on it?
It's yours — the video you put in and everything Reissue makes from it. We don't use your content to train shared models, and we don't sell or share it. Reissue learns your voice for you, not for anyone else.
What does it cost?
Pricing lands closer to launch. The founding 100 lock in the best rate we'll ever offer and keep it for good — that's the whole point of getting in early.
